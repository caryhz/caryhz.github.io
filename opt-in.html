Who may opt in

Only the business owner (Cary Hernandez) may receive messages from this service. There is no public signup. No marketing list. No third-party recipients.

What you are opting in to

Optional SMS account notifications when a publicly published U.S. 30-year mortgage rate (Mortgage News Daily daily survey) falls below a threshold configured by the owner. Messages are transactional/personal alerts, not promotions.

How opt-in works

  1. The owner decides to enable personal mortgage rate SMS alerts.
  2. The owner voluntarily provides their own mobile number in private account configuration (Twilio and automation settings).
  3. No message is sent until alerts are enabled and a threshold condition occurs.

Message frequency

At most one SMS per day when an alert condition is met; often zero messages.

Sample alert message

Mortgage alert: MND 30Y fixed is 6.250% (below threshold 6.500%). Prior row: -0.020. ~Week: -0.110.

Opt-out

The owner may stop messages at any time by disabling alerts or removing their phone number from configuration. Reply STOP to any message to opt out (handled by carrier/Twilio).
